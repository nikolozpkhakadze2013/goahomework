Example of album

An album is a collection of audio recordings issued on a physical medium such as compact disc (CD), vinyl, audio tape, or digital distribution. It is usually associated with a single artist or band, and is usually composed of several tracks. Albums typically contain between 10 and 12 songs, with some albums having as many as 18 tracks. They are typically designed to be listened to as a complete body of work. Albums are typically collections of songs that are from one artist.
