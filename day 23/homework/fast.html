About:

Brian O'Conner is an LAPD officer who is assigned to investigate a series of thefts in which a group of thieves, driving three black Honda Civic Coupes (EJ1) assault trucks loaded with valuable goods. The first of the suspects is Dominic Toretto, whom O'Conner has been following undercover for some time.
